About Gems Akademia International School

Welcome to the vibrant community of Gems Akademia International School (GAIS),in Kolkata. Established to make children well-rounded and self-dependent individuals, the school has been constructed on a sprawling 20 acre campus with splendid infrastructural facilities in a tranquil and pollution free environment.

Every day our teachers and staff strive to help students aged between 2+ & 18, from all backgrounds, cultures and educational levels to learn, thrive and grow. The entire gamut of activities in GAIS reflects its objective of nurturing young children and making them responsible adults who can make a positive contribution to the world.
